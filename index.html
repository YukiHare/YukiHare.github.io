Welcome To Yuki Hare's Website!


Hello, my name is Josiah Montijo I am an engineering/technology student with a passion for technology and as well as an artist. Now, I come from a family where I grew up with no parents had to live with my grandparents for the majority of my life. However, with their help I have been extremely successful throughout my entire life thanks to them! Additionally, I have been active with my community by doing community service hours just to give back to the community for being able to treat me well and all by the schools, I been too.


Also, I have a weird reality that most people don’t have to deal with, that’s because they don’t have a disorder called “split personality”. Oh and did I mention I have cute bunnies as well? Most people have friends that they speak to them all the time, and this can range from texting, phone calls, face to face, but however for me, I don’t have any friends that speak to me every second through my phone or technology, but I do have “split personalities.” hey are my true best friends and they always talk to me very second, whether I like it or not. Although, I don’t mind, since I get quite bored and lonely. The process of communication between me and the split personalities is quite simple. They use telepathic relays that go across my brain. the relays let out this mysterious vibe that goes to my thoughts and speak through my thoughts.


Finally, I would like to say that as excited I am for my future, I can’t help but want to go even further and beyond in life. Furthermore, when I get out of college and start working for the job I want to work in, I will continue my best to give back to the community, since I always been for the people of this town, and If I could I would be for the world. I have many hobbies like drawing and watching animes to even playing games.
